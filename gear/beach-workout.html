Beach
Workout Gear
Sand makes everything harder. That's the point. Build a portable gym that goes wherever the water is.

Train where you'd rather be anyway

The beach is the best gym you'll ever have access to — and it's free. Sand adds resistance to every movement, the uneven surface fires up stabilizer muscles you didn't know existed, and the salt air makes it all feel less like work.

You don't need much. A set of resistance bands, a sandbag, and some bodyweight programming will give you a full-body workout that demolishes anything you'd do inside. The trick is having gear that can take the abuse — sand, saltwater, sun — and still perform.

We find gear built for the beach, not just gear that happens to work at the beach.

Sand = Free Resistance
Running, lunging, and jumping on sand burns 30% more calories than the same movements on a hard surface. Your ankles will hate you. Your glutes will thank you.
Portable Is Everything
The best beach workout gear fits in a backpack. Resistance bands, a sandbag, a jump rope. Total weight under 10 lbs. Total workout potential: unlimited.
Salt & Sand Resistant
Not all gear survives the beach. Look for rust-proof hardware, sealed stitching, and materials that rinse clean. Cheap gear deteriorates fast in saltwater environments.
