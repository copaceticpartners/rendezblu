How to choose

Native waterproofing vs housing. Modern GoPro, Insta360, and DJI cameras are natively waterproof to 10m without a housing. Older cameras require a waterproof housing that adds bulk and can fog up. Always check the native waterproofing spec before buying.

Mounts make or break water sports footage. The camera is only as good as the mount. Chest mounts for SUP and surfing, jaw/chin mounts for helmets, board mounts for underwater perspective. GoPro's magnetic mount ecosystem is the most versatile. Invest in good mounts.

Stabilization matters more than resolution. A 5.3K clip with poor stabilization is worse than a 4K clip with excellent stabilization. HyperSmooth 7.0 and DJI's RockSteady are both excellent. Prioritize stabilization spec over raw resolution numbers.

Bring extra batteries. Action cams drain fast in cold water. A session in 55°F water will drain a battery in 45–60 minutes. Always bring at least one spare battery and a waterproof charging case.
