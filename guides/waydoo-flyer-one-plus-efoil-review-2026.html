How to choose

Try before you commit. Every reputable eFoil dealer offers demo days and lessons. A 2-hour lesson runs $150-250 and tells you everything you need to know about whether eFoiling is for you and which board feels right. Do not spend $6,000+ without riding first.

The learning curve is real but short. Most people achieve sustained flight within 2-3 sessions with proper instruction. The first session involves a lot of falling. By session 3, most riders are flying comfortably. By session 10, carving and speed control feel natural. The curve is steep but short.

Battery logistics are part of the experience. A 90-120 minute session, 2-3 hour charge time. If you want all-day riding, you need a second battery or plan for a mid-day charge break. Factor the charging infrastructure into your setup — a 30-amp outlet charges significantly faster than standard household current.

Foiling regulations vary by location. Some beach areas restrict eFoils due to noise or safety concerns — always check local regulations before your first session. eFoils are generally quieter than jet skis and jet boards, making them more welcome in sensitive areas, but rules vary significantly by jurisdiction.

Our bottom line
The Waydoo Flyer ONE Plus is the right starting point for most people entering eFoiling — the price-to-performance ratio is genuinely compelling and the 120-minute ride time beats more expensive competitors. Ready to step up performance? Waydoo EVO Pro Plus for carving, SiFly Stellar for tech features. Want to stay on the surface at maximum speed? The 20KW Carbon Fiber Jet Board is a completely different but equally thrilling experience.
